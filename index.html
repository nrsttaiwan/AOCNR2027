Welcome Message / 歡迎詞

大會主席致詞

大會主席: 張豐基 (Feng-Chi Chang)

各位神經學及神經影像的專家及朋友大家好：

個人謹代表大會，誠摯的邀請您參加 2027 年在台北舉辦的第 16 屆亞洲太平洋神經放射線會議。

台北是一個傳統與科技，文化與美食互相融會的新興都市，在巡禮參與會議可讓您充分的體驗到這個都市的充沛活力，而這場盛會也會邀請到全球頂尖的神經學及神經影像的專家，共同探究及分享神經放射性學的最新進展與創新。我們誠摯邀請您參加我們的活動及會議，體驗這場豐富的學術與文化交流，熱切期待在台北迎接您的蒞臨。

Dear colleagues and friends,

On behalf of the Organizing Committee, it is my great pleasure to invite you to the 16th Asian-Oceanian Congress of Neuroradiology (AOCNR), which will be held in the vibrant city of Taipei, Taiwan, in 2027.

Taipei is not only a meeting point of technology and culture but also a capital of gourmet food, allowing you to fully enjoy the city's warmth and convenience alongside your academic exchange. This prestigious event promises to bring together leading experts, researchers, and practitioners from around the globe to share the latest advancements and innovations in the field of neuroradiology. Join us to experience this rich academic and cultural exchange. We look forward to welcoming you to Taipei!

Warm regards,

Feng-Chi Chang (張豐基)

Congress President, 2027 Taipei AOCNR

President, Neuroradiologic Society of Taiwan (中華民國神經放射線醫學會 理事長)
